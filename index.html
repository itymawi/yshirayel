About
Yshi

I'm a senior psychology student who is genuinely excited by understanding people — what drives them, what shapes them, and how good systems can help them flourish.

I organize things. A lot. Events, manuals, task trackers, project proposals — if there's structure to be made, I'll make it. I've learned that good documentation is an act of care.

I'm also a violinist, which means I know what it means to show up consistently for something bigger than yourself — and that grit sounds better when you add grace to it.

Currently sharpening my project management skills (Google-certified, counting), and always looking for meaningful work to jump into.
